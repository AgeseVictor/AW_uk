About Us

We are the United Kingdom Community of Ambassadors Word Ministry, a Spirit led people birthed with the mission to reach the unreached with the gospel, and raise through God’s word, a people that are whole, perfect and mature in all areas of life. We are particularly taken with music, dinning on and spreading God’s word and building a community of people that truly love, excel greatly and bask in the joy of God.
